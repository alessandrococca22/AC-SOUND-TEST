About

With a proven track record in high-profile studio environments, my approach combines deep technical knowledge with "artistic empathy" — prioritizing the artist's vision and emotional state to translate abstract creative goals into tangible sonic results.

Holding a BA (Hons) in Audio Production from the University of Hertfordshire/SAE Institute and expanding my expertise in Live Sound Engineering at the ICMP Institute.

I have provided technical support at Signature Studios, for top-tier UK artists, including D-Block Europe (on their Spotify #1 Debut PTSD 2).

My studio experience includes operating industry-standard consoles such as the SSL G+, SSL AWS 900, and Neve VR Legend.

Beyond the mixing desk, I am an experienced freelancer in production, specializing in studio session, mixing and mastering. Whether I am managing complex recording sessions or refining audio assets for live performance, I bring a calm demeanor under pressure and a proactive commitment to audio fidelity.

Professional audio that captures every dimension of sound — from the studio to the stage, for music, film, and live events. Front-of-house to the final mix.
